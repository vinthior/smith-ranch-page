    Some good places to get information on different goat breeds:

  • The American goat federation

    • This site is good for different types of goats your looking for. Be it milk, fiber, meat, or just for brush control.

  • OSU's site for breed information

    • This give you a visual of all the different breed types around the world and history of the different breeds.

  • The U.S.D.A auction reports

    • A market report for goat auction prices by state.

  • Savanna spectacular

    • The Savanna spectacular is a good place for purebreed auction and many seminars for breeders intrested in breeding goats. This is normally a three day event in Missouri where many breeders congregate and exchange information.
